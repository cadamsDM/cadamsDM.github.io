Our Story

Fashion-forward second-hand shopping for Christchurch's trendsetters

ThimbleWinter: Fashion Reimagined

Founded in 2020, ThimbleWinter began as a small community of fashion enthusiasts in Christchurch who believed that style shouldn't come at the expense of individuality. We saw a gap in the market for curated second-hand fashion that appealed to the trend-conscious consumer.

Our platform connects fashion-forward individuals who want to refresh their wardrobes with unique pieces that tell a story. We focus on seasonal collections that align with Christchurch's distinctive climate and style sensibilities.

Unlike typical second-hand marketplaces, ThimbleWinter is carefully curated to ensure only the most fashionable, high-quality pieces make it to our platform. Our team of style experts hand-selects featured items and works with sellers to showcase their best pieces.

Join our community of fashion lovers who believe that great style is timeless, not disposable. Discover unique pieces that will make your wardrobe stand out from the crowd.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shopping and selling on ThimbleWinter

How do I buy an item on ThimbleWinter?

To purchase an item, simply click on the product you're interested in, select your size if applicable, and click "Add to Cart." You can then proceed to checkout where you'll enter your shipping and payment information. Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive a confirmation email, and the seller will be notified to ship your item.

How do I sell items on ThimbleWinter?

To start selling, create an account and click "Sell" in your dashboard. You'll need to upload clear photos of your item, write a detailed description, set your price, and select shipping options. Once your listing is approved, it will appear on the marketplace. When someone purchases your item, you'll receive a notification and can ship it directly to the buyer.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Afterpay for your convenience. All transactions are secure and encrypted to protect your financial information.

Can I return an item if it doesn't fit or I don't like it?

Returns are handled on a case-by-case basis and are at the discretion of individual sellers. We encourage buyers to ask detailed questions about measurements and condition before making a purchase. If an item arrives not as described, please contact us within 48 hours, and we'll help mediate with the seller.

How does shipping work?

Each seller sets their own shipping rates and methods. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within Christchurch. You can track your order through your account dashboard once the seller has shipped your item. For international shipping, please check with individual sellers as not all offer this service.

How do I know the items are authentic?

While we can't guarantee authenticity for all items, we have a verification process for high-value designer pieces. Additionally, our community rating system allows buyers to leave feedback about their purchases, helping maintain trust and transparency on the platform. If you have concerns about a specific item, we recommend asking the seller for additional photos or proof of purchase.
